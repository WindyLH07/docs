[Katalon licenses](https://docs.katalon.com/katalon-studio/docs/license.html) are purchased and managed via **Organizations** in [Katalon TestOps](https://admin.katalon.com/). Only the Organization Owner or Billing Managers can subscribe to Katalon licenses.

If you need further assistance with Katalon Pricing and Subscription, contact our sales team via business@katalon.com.

## Create your Katalon Organization

To subscribe to any Katalon license, you need a Katalon account and a Katalon Organization.

* To regist a Katalon account, go to [Katalon](https://www.katalon.com/sign-up/).

* To create an Organization with your Katalon account, log into [Katalon TestOps](https://admin.katalon.com/). Click **Create Organization**, fill in your Organization name, then click **Create**.

    Since you create this Organization, you are granted the Owner role of this Organization by default. Learn more about managing users and roles at [User Management](https://docs.katalon.com/katalon-analytics/docs/user-management.html).

## Get a Price Quote for Annual Subscription

Katalon provides monthly and annual subscriptions. For an annual subscription, you can get a Price Quote before purchasing your licenses. If you are a monthly subscriber or don't need a Price Quote for an annual subscription, you can skip to [Purchase your Licenses](https://docs.katalon.com/katalon-studio/docs/license-subscription.html#purchase-your-licenses).

Follow these steps to get a Price Quote and pay for the Quote:

1. Log into [Katalon TestOps](https://admin.katalon.com/).
2. Select your desired Organization and select **Subscribe to Katalon products**. The **Subscriptions** page appears.
3. Select **Studio Subscription**. In the **Billing Summary**, select the license you want to get a Quote, then click **Get Quote**.
    <img src="https://github.com/katalon-studio/docs-images/raw/master/katalon-studio/docs/license-subscription/katalon-testops.png" alt="billing summary" width=100%>
4. Specify the billing cycle and the number of licenses of each product you want to get a Quote.
    <img src="https://github.com/katalon-studio/docs-images/raw/master/katalon-studio/docs/license-subscription/Get-quote.png" alt="get quote" width=100%>
5. Apply discount codes (if any).
6. Click **Get Quote**. An email will be sent to you automatically, which contains a checkout link and a Price Quote.
7. In the Price Quote email, click on the checkout link. You or a person in charge can pay for this Quote.

> Note:
>
> You can also get Quote via our website [Katalon Pricing](https://katalon.com/pricing). Click **Get a Price Quote** and fill in your information. A quote with details about products and pricing will be generated.
>
> <img src="https://github.com/katalon-studio/docs-images/raw/master/katalon-studio/docs/license-subscription/katalon-pricing.png" alt="get a price quote" alt=100%>
>
> You cannot check out via Quote generated from Katalon Pricing website. If you want to have a Price Quote with a checkout link, please create a Quote in Katalon Admin.

## Purchase your Licenses

Follow these steps to purchase licenses:

1. Log into [Katalon TestOps](https://admin.katalon.com/).
2. Select your desired Organization and select **Subscribe to Katalon products**. The **Subscriptions** page appears.
    <img src="https://github.com/katalon-studio/docs-images/raw/master/katalon-studio/docs/license-subscription/purchase.png" alt="purchase" width=100%>

    > Notes:
    >
    > You can also navigate to the Katalon TestOps Subscription page from Katalon Pricing Websites by following these steps:
    >
    > * Log into [Katalon Pricing](https://www.katalon.com/pricing/).
    > * Locate your desired products and click **Buy Now**.
    > * Select your Organization. You're navigated to the [Katalon TestOps Subscription](https://admin.katalon.com/) page.

3. Select **Studio Subscription**. In the **Billing Summary**, select the license you want to purchase, then click **Subscribe**.
4. Specify the billing cycle and the number of licenses for each product you want to purchase.
5. Apply discount codes (if any).
6. In the **Checkout** section, enter your card details or use a saved card to check out.
7. In **Billing Information**, you can enter some extra billing information that you want to display on the receipts. When you finish, click **Save Billing Information**.
8. To pay for your order, click **Checkout**.

> Notes:
>
> After successfully purchase a Katalon license, we recommend you should:
>
> * Verify licenses information. See [Manage Katalon License](https://docs.katalon.com/katalon-studio/docs/license-management.html#verify-and-view-licenses-information).
> * Make sure to re-activate your Katalon account to begin using the purchased license.

## Subscribe via Purchase Order

If you have successfully subscribed to KSE and RE via Purchase Order (PO) method, the licenses are generated and added to your account by the Katalon team.

Every Katalon license is associated with an Organization in Katalon Admin. Please follow these steps to complete the subscription process:

1. Log in to [Katalon TestOps](https://admin.katalon.com/).
2. Select your desired Organization.
3. Send the Organization's URL containing its ID and the Organization Owner's email to the Katalon team.

## Subscription Renewal

Your subscription will automatically renew on the specified day included in your **Order Summary** email. You will not be charged if you cancel your subscription before that day.
